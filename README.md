# The Lighthouse Letters

A daily serialized cozy mystery, drawn by AI.

A 28-year-old woman inherits her grandmother's lighthouse cottage in coastal Maine. On her first quiet morning, a sealed bottle washes up on the rocks below the keeper's house — addressed to her by name. The letter inside describes something that hasn't happened yet.

The locals say her grandmother used to *answer* the letters that came in bottles. Nobody can quite explain how.

---

## About this repository

This is the **source archive** for the comic serial — every episode's original prose, character reference photos, and links to the rendered comic pages live here. The series itself is posted daily to Instagram, Reddit, and LinkedIn.

**Why public?** Because every episode is built by feeding the prose through a story engine I built — [BubbleStory](https://bubblestory.io/?utm_source=github&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=cozy-mystery). Anyone curious about how a 2,850-word short story becomes a 10-page Risograph comic can read the input here, then look at the rendered output and judge the engine for themselves. Full transparency, both directions.

## The engine

Every comic in this archive is the output of [BubbleStory](https://bubblestory.io/) — a story engine that:
- **Reads** prose (any text — fiction, journals, lyrics, scripts)
- **Decides** which moments deserve a page and how the story paces
- **Imagines** each visual scene at panel level
- **Renders** the result as a multi-page comic in 1 of 91 art styles, with character likeness preserved across every style

The Lighthouse Letters is a 14-day public stress test of the engine: one episode per day, posted to three channels, all source prose published here under full transparency.
